About Haiklu

Discover the captivating world of Haiklu, where poetry meets puzzle in a unique and stimulating gaming experience. In Haiklu, you'll immerse yourself in the beauty of haikus, those mesmerizing three-line poems that capture the essence of a moment.

Gameplay

But here's the twist: you won't just admire these poetic gems; you'll interact with them! Haiklu challenges you to solve a 4-word crossword puzzle that complements the haiku's theme, adding a layer of creativity and wordplay to the mix. Whether you're a word enthusiast or a poetry lover, Haiklu will test your linguistic prowess and ignite your imagination.
